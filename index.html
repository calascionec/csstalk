BEM

BEM is an abbreviation for the 3 elements of it's naming methodology: Block, Element, Modifier.

Benefits:

  • Modularity
  • Reusability
  • Structure
http://getbem.com/

Block

Standalone entity that is meaningful on it's own. Blocks can exist inside other blocks but they are not dependent on any other blocks or elements on the page.

Button
http://getbem.com/naming/

Element

Part of a block and have no standalone meaning.

http://getbem.com/naming/

Modifier

Flags placed on blocks or elements that change their appearance, behaviour, or state.
